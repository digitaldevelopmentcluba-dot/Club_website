The Game Coding Club, also known as the Digital Development Club, is a self-directed community focused on digital development. We support members by providing:

  • Guidance and mentorship for learning programming and game development
  • Resources and tools to help you build projects
  • Collaboration opportunities with other developers
  • A shared GitHub space for open-source club projects that any member can contribute to

Our goal is to help members learn, create, and grow through hands-on development and community support or just supply a digital place to hangout.
