Hello, welcome to the game coding club also know as digital development club front page.

For those who just want a basic summary of what we do and why someone would join the club, here it is:

The Game Coding Club, also known as the Digital Development Club, is a self-directed community where we help you achieve your goals related to digital development. Whether you're looking to learn, create, or collaborate, we provide guidance, resources, and support to help you along the way

When it comes to projects, we don’t impose any control unless the project is hosted on the official club GitHub account. If a project is on the GitHub, it becomes a shared club project, meaning all members can contribute, modify, and collaborate on it. These projects will also be open-source, allowing anyone to learn and build upon them.

If you love coding, want to learn, or simply enjoy hanging out with like-minded individuals, you’re welcome to join us! To get started, just join our Discord server using the code: [ mRqCMxtjXW ].
